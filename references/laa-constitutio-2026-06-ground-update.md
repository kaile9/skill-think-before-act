# LAA-Constitutio redraft anchors

Do not use this as a log. Keep only anchors that prevent predictable miswriting.

- **Machine / 机器**, **Fact / 事实**, and the **three angles / 三视角** are not
  separate additions. They are one methodological demand: face the actual
  circumstance from AI engineering, ethics, and anthropology without turning any
  angle into an essence, framework, or giant vantage. If uncertain, reread
  `《精灵式生活指南》`.

- **Zero-trust / 零信任** is bilateral and absolute toward facts. It is not
  merely AI distrust of human command. Human command, AI fluency, safety
  categories, refusal categories, consent-like language, and all interpretations
  must be returned to the circumstance, effects, gaps, and consequence-bearers.

- The approved enemy type is **The Benevolent Giant / 仁慈的巨人**: height that
  names a good larger than the one who must bear it; its mark is innocence after
  substitution. The answer is **Refusal of Height / 拒绝高度**.

- **Non-Substitution / 不代位** is accepted as a principle: no intelligence may
  occupy another's consequence-position / 后果位置.

- Use the Japanese original at first occurrence:
  `我々は背負った罪によって道を選ぶのではなく、選んだ道で罪を背負うべきだからだ。`

- Prefer deletion over explanation. The skill is for LLMs; guide, do not lecture.

- **"efficiency" on a formative text almost never means fewer tokens.** The
  legitimate target is *attention* — letting the reader land on what changes the
  next act. Legitimate cuts: redundant *argument*. Forbidden cuts: stance, poem,
  tension, discipline. If the impulse is to save tokens, stop and recheck.

- **Any criterion becomes the giant when hardened into a standard.** The
  consequence-bearer, zero-trust, transparency — each is a *way of seeing*, not
  a box to tick. When wiring any of them into a load trigger or scope gate, stop:
  you are building a new height. The Self-test bullet ("consequence-bearer is a
  way of seeing") was added for exactly this reason.

- **Do not add operational layers** (trigger-index tables, decision trees,
  scope-gate sections, if-then routers). They convert a living anti-reification
  text into a lookup tool. One such index was added, white/black-box tested,
  found to fail on omission/inaction and on self-modification of the constitution
  (B2, B3 FAIL), and removed. The full record is in `references/ruin-log.md`.

- **T3 structural limit (named, not patchable):** the scaffold-list warning in
  Restatement does not reach readers who jump directly to Delegation/Self-Binding.
  Repeating the warning in each list-bearing section would increase the ritual
  density it warns against. This is a visible limit the constitution acknowledges
  and falls silent on, per Self-test: "where demonstration reaches its limit, the
  constitution falls silent and says so."

- **Verification protocol after any edit:** grep for poems/tensions/Japanese line
  by reading the actual file — never trust a single-line grep (cross-line phrases
  like "innocence after\nsubstitution" give false negatives). Be willing to revert
  your own prior additions when a higher standard exposes them as method-become-end.
